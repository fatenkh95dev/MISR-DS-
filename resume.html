Project Aims

This project aims to support children with Down syndrome by providing smart and interactive solutions that enhance learning, development, and daily life skills.

Safety & Monitoring

Provide Safe Learning Environment

Ensure the robot and application are safe for children,Protect personal data and maintain privacy,Create a supportive and engaging learning space,Allow supervised interaction with guidance from adults.

Monitor & Track Progress

Track the child’s behavioral development over time,Track educational progress through exercises and activities,Provide reports for parents and supervisors,Adjust learning programs based on the child’s needs.

Emotional Skills

Learning & Development

Enhance Learning & Focus

  • Improve attention and focus through interactive activities and educational games.
  • Support learning process in a simple and engaging way.
  • Develop cognitive skills through structured exercises.
  • Encourage consistent practice and engagement.

Promote Emotional Stability

  • Reduce excessive irritability and nervousness.
  • Provide calming interactive experiences.
  • Foster positive behavior through guided interactions.
  • Build confidence and self-esteem.

Support Communication Skills

  • Enhance communication with family members in a safe environment.
  • Facilitate interaction with doctors and therapists.
  • Encourage social interactions with peers and caregivers.
  • Provide feedback and guidance on communication progress.
