ABOUT OUR PROJECT

This project presents an intelligent robot designed to support and assist individuals with Down Syndrome. The website simulates the graduation project and explains its concept, goals, and impact

Hello there

Hi, am MISR DS, I was designed to support children with Down Syndrome who face challenges in communication, learning, and daily interaction.

I provide interactive activities, educational exercises, and emotional support to help improve cognitive and social skills, while offering continuous and specialized care.

I also adapt to each child’s individual needs by encouraging positive interaction and gradual learning in a safe and friendly environment. My goal is to support daily development, build confidence, and assist families and specialists in providing better care and guidance.
