Services

We provide smart and interactive solutions that help children with Down syndrome learn, practice daily skills, and improve their abilities through technology and guided activities.

Web Platform Service

Providing an accessible and user-friendly website that showcases the project, displays progress data, and supports interaction between the robot system, the application, and parents or supervisors. The platform is designed to be clear, responsive, and easy to navigate for all users..

Learn More

Mobile App Assistance

A smart application designed to track the child’s progress, display interactive exercises, and guide the child step by step. It helps parents and supervisors monitor performance and adjust activities based on the child’s needs.

Learn More

Child-Centered UI/UX Design

Designing interactive and easy-to-use interfaces tailored to children with Down syndrome. The user experience focuses on simplicity, clarity, and engagement to support learning and encourage positive interaction..

Learn More

Progress Monitoring

Tracking the child’s level and development over time to help parents and supervisors evaluate improvement. The system provides clear insights into performance, highlights strengths and areas that need support, and helps adjust training activities for better results..

Learn More

Skill Development Programs

Programs designed to enhance children’s motor, cognitive, and social skills through tailored exercises and interactive activities. These programs help children practice essential daily tasks, build confidence, and improve their learning abilities in a structured and engaging way.

Learn More

Supportive & Safe Environment

Providing a safe and comfortable learning environment tailored to children’s abilities and needs. This environment includes interactive tools and engaging activities that encourage children to learn and practice confidently, with continuous support from supervisors or the application to ensure a positive and secure educational experience.
